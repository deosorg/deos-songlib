rnotes is a songlib program that generates 8 random notes having
random duration.

Examples:

    rnotes

        - generates 8 major notes (C,D,E,F,G,A,B)

    rnotes 1234

        - as above but reseeds the random number generator with 1234

    rnotes -m0 

        - generates 8 major and minor notes (C,C#,D,D#,...)

    rnotes -m0 1234

        - as above but reseeds the random number generator with 1234
